About Us

Kids Club Kampala (KCK) is a Christian-based charity organization founded in 2009 by three friends: Sam, Olivia, and Corrie. We are registered in Uganda under NGO number MIA/NB/2010/04/2497, the United Kingdom, and the Republic of Ireland.

Our Vision

A world in which hope and love for vulnerable children transforms communities.

Join Us

Our Mission

Availing children safe places to play while catering for their physical, emotional, and spiritual needs, and empowering them to realise their God-given potential.

Where We Work

We currently work in 10 Kampala slums and other parts of Uganda, running early childhood education projects, skilling centers, transitional homes, and safeguarding programs.
